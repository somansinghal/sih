Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Garudyaan.

How accurate is the real-time tracking?

Our real-time tracking system provides 99.9% accuracy with updates every 30 seconds. We use advanced GPS technology and work directly with transport authorities to ensure the most accurate data.

Which cities are currently supported?

We currently support 12 major Tier 2 cities including Indore, Bhopal, Nagpur, Coimbatore, Kochi, Mysore, Jaipur, Lucknow, Ahmedabad, Pune, Chennai, and Hyderabad. We're constantly expanding to more cities.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, Garudyaan is completely free to use. We believe in making public transport accessible to everyone. Our revenue comes from partnerships with transport authorities and local businesses.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

You can report bugs or suggest features through our contact form, email us at support@garudyaan.com, or use the feedback page. We review all suggestions and implement the most requested features.

Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, we offer a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works like a native app on mobile devices. You can install it from your browser and use it offline. Native mobile apps for Android and iOS are in development.

How do I add my city to Garudyaan?

We're always looking to expand to new cities. If you're a transport authority or city official, please contact us at partnerships@garudyaan.com. We work closely with local governments to integrate their transport systems.
