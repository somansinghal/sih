Features Guide

Get the most out of Garudyaan with these tips

Changing Themes & Languages

Personalize your experience by selecting your preferred theme and language. You can find these options in the top-right corner of the navigation bar on every page.

1. Locate the Selectors

Look for the theme (☀️) and language (🇺🇸 EN) buttons in the navigation bar.

2. Open the Menu

Click on either button to open a dropdown menu with available options.

3. Make Your Selection

Click on your desired theme (Light, Dark, Tricolor) or language. The website will update instantly.

Using the Favorites Modal

Save your most-used routes for quick and easy access. You need to be logged in to use this feature.

1. Add a Route to Favorites

When viewing a route, click the "Add to Favorites" button or the heart icon.

2. Access Your Favorites

Click the heart icon () in the navigation bar to open the favorites modal.

3. Manage Your Favorites

From the modal, you can quickly track a route or remove it from your favorites list. Your favorites are also available on your User Dashboard.

Sharing Route Information

Easily share route details with friends and family so they can track a journey or plan a trip.

1. Select a Route

First, select a route on the Track page to view its details.

2. Find the Share Button

In the selected route's information panel, you will find a "Share" button.

3. Share or Copy Link

Clicking the button will open your device's native share menu. If that's not available, it will copy a link to your clipboard that you can paste anywhere.
