Popular Questions

How accurate is the real-time tracking?

Our real-time tracking system provides 99.9% accuracy with updates every 30 seconds. We use advanced GPS technology and work directly with transport authorities to ensure the most accurate data.

Which cities are currently supported?

We currently support over 12 major Tier 2 cities and are constantly expanding. You can see the full list on our Cities page.

Is the Garudyaan app free to use?

Yes, Garudyaan is completely free for all users. We are committed to making public transport more accessible for everyone.

Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, we offer a Progressive Web App (PWA) that you can add to your home screen for an app-like experience. Native Android and iOS apps are in development.
