Troubleshooting Guide

Find solutions to common issues and get back on track.

The map is not loading or shows an error.

This can happen for a few reasons. Please try the following steps:

  • Check your internet connection: A stable connection is required to load map data.
  • Reload the page: A simple refresh can often resolve temporary glitches.
  • Clear your browser cache: Outdated data in your cache can sometimes cause issues.
  • If the problem persists, it might be a temporary issue with our map provider. Please try again after some time or contact support.

I'm having trouble logging in or registering.

If you're facing login or registration issues, consider the following:

  • Forgot Password: Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset it.
  • Incorrect Credentials: Double-check that your email and password are correct.
  • Account Not Registered: If you're new, make sure you have completed the registration process on the register page.
  • If you're still unable to access your account, please contact our support team for assistance.

The route information seems incorrect or outdated.

We strive to provide the most accurate data, but sometimes discrepancies can occur. Our data is sourced directly from local transport authorities.

  • Data Sync Lag: There might be a slight delay between updates from the authorities and our system.
  • Report the Issue: If you notice persistent inaccuracies, please help us by reporting the issue through our feedback page or contacting us directly. Provide the city, route number, and the incorrect information.
