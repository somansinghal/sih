Quick Start Guide

  1. Click "Launch Website" to open the main application
  2. Select a City from 12+ supported Tier 2 cities
  3. Browse Routes available in your selected city
  4. Click on a Route to start live tracking
  5. Use Search to find specific routes quickly
  6. Filter by Type (Bus, Train, Auto) for better results
  7. Change Language using the language selector (Hindi, Tamil, etc.)
  8. Switch Themes between Light, Dark, and Tricolor modes
  9. Add to Favorites for quick access to your preferred routes
  10. Share Routes with friends and family
