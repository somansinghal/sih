About Garudyaan

Revolutionizing public transport tracking across India

"Empowering Every Journey"

"Technology Meets Transportation"

"Building Smart Cities, One Route at a Time"

Our Mission

To make public transport accessible, reliable, and trackable for every Indian citizen. We believe that efficient public transportation is the backbone of a developing nation, and technology should make it easier for people to navigate their cities.

Why Garudyaan?

The name "Garudyaan" is inspired by Garuda, the divine eagle in Hindu mythology known for its speed and reliability. Just like Garuda, our platform ensures fast and dependable transport tracking services.
